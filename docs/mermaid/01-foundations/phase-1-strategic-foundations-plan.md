# Phase 01 – Strategic Foundations (OGC NewFinity Platform)

## Objective

Establish the strategic direction and scope of the OGC NewFinity Platform.

## Key Elements

- Vision: A token-powered ecosystem that merges AI, blockchain, and development tools.  
- Mission: Enable innovation, Contribution-Based Mining, and digital empowerment through a unified platform.  
- Core Values: Transparency, decentralization, collaboration, creativity, and sustainability.  
- Ecosystem Pillars:
  1. **Platform Core:** User dashboards, OGC Wallet (→ OGC Vault), Amy AI Agent integration.
  2. **OGCFinity Token Economy:** Two-phase model (Genesis on Polygon, Native on OGC Chain) - Reward and governance foundation for the entire ecosystem.
  3. **OGC NewFinity Challenge Program:** Three official tracks, funded by Innovation & Sustainability Challenge Fund (20% – 100M OGCFinity).
  4. **Amy AI Agent:** Smart automation and productivity engine.
  5. **Wallet System:** OGC Wallet → OGC Vault evolution, staking, transactions, and blockchain integration.

- Alignment document with token governance (Phase 06)
