# OGC NewFinity — Contribution-Mining Governance Rules (v1.0)

## 1. Introduction

This document defines the governance rules, participation standards, reward mechanisms, and oversight processes for the Contribution-Mining System within OGC NewFinity.

Contribution Mining allows users to earn OGC rewards by:

- Creating value
- Participating in challenges
- Supporting the community
- Contributing to platform growth

This governance framework ensures fairness, transparency, anti-abuse protection, and long-term sustainability of the reward ecosystem.

## 2. Objectives of Contribution Mining

### 2.1 System Goals

- Reward meaningful contributions
- Encourage high-quality participation
- Promote community engagement
- Support education and creativity
- Incentivize platform growth
- Maintain a balanced token economy

### 2.2 Governance Goals

- Prevent abuse
- Ensure fair scoring
- Maintain transparency
- Support scalable reward distribution

## 3. Contribution Categories

### 3.1 Challenge Participation

Users earn rewards for:

- Submissions
- High-quality entries
- Winning or placing in challenges

### 3.2 Community Support

Rewards may be granted for:

- Helping other users
- Providing verified answers
- Contributing to knowledge-base improvements

### 3.3 Creative & Platform Contributions

Includes:

- Publishing educational materials
- Suggesting approved feature ideas
- Contributing to platform content (future)

### 3.4 Special Events

Seasonal or event-based reward opportunities.

## 4. Contribution Scoring Model

### 4.1 Scoring Components

Each contribution is evaluated based on:

- Quality (clarity, creativity, skill)
- Impact (benefit to community/platform)
- Effort Level
- Verification (reviewed by moderators/judges)

### 4.2 Score Range

Scores typically range:

- 0–100 for standard contributions
- 0–200 for premium challenge events

### 4.3 Score Multipliers

Available for:

- Younger participants
- New users onboarding
- High-impact contributions
- Seasonal events

## 5. Reward Distribution Rules

### 5.1 Core Reward Types

- OGCFinity
- Badges
- Certificates
- Ranking points

### 5.2 Reward Pools

Two pools may exist:

- Daily/Weekly Reward Pool
- Seasonal Reward Pool

### 5.3 Distribution Logic

Rewards are calculated based on:

- Contribution score
- Difficulty of challenge
- Category weight
- Pool capacity

### 5.4 Reward Visibility

Users must be able to see:

- Earned rewards
- Pending rewards
- Contribution breakdowns
- Score calculation explanations

## 6. Anti-Abuse & Fraud Prevention

### 6.1 Prohibited Behavior

- Plagiarism
- Duplicate submissions
- Auto-generated content spam
- Inflated scoring via collusion
- Attempting to manipulate rankings
- Creating multiple accounts

### 6.2 Penalties

Violations may result in:

- Score reduction
- Reward removal
- Temporary bans
- Permanent suspension

### 6.3 Detection Systems

Includes:

- Automated plagiarism detection
- Pattern-based fraud checks
- Moderator reviews
- AI-based anomaly detection (future)

## 7. Verification & Review Process

### 7.1 Reviewers

Reviewers evaluate:

- Submission quality
- Validity of contribution
- Compliance with rules

### 7.2 Review Process Steps

- Submission received
- Automated checks applied
- Reviewer verification
- Score assigned
- Reward calculated
- Reward distributed

### 7.3 Reviewer Accountability

All reviewer actions are logged.

## 8. Community Governance in Contribution Mining

### 8.1 Community Input

Users may submit:

- Proposals to revise scoring models
- Ideas for new contribution categories
- Suggestions for seasonal events

### 8.2 Voting (Future)

Community voting may influence:

- Reward pool adjustments
- Category weighting
- New contribution types

### 8.3 Reputation-Based Influence (Future)

High-reputation users may gain:

- Reviewer privileges
- Governance influence

## 9. Transparency Requirements

### 9.1 Public Documentation

The following must be publicly available:

- Scoring rules
- Reward formulas
- Pool sizes
- Anti-abuse rules

### 9.2 Public Logs (Future)

Will include:

- Contribution audits
- Reward distribution summaries
- Seasonal adjustments

## 10. Economic Sustainability Rules

### 10.1 Protection Measures

- Reward pools capped by system rules
- Anti-inflation controls
- Sustainable reward pacing

### 10.2 Token Sink Mechanics (Future)

Possible mechanisms:

- Marketplace fees
- Premium features
- Upgrades
- Challenge entry boosts

### 10.3 Oversight

Governance Council evaluates:

- Economic impact
- Token distribution fairness
- Long-term sustainability

## 11. Future Enhancements

- Dynamic scoring algorithms
- On-chain contribution records (future)
- Reputation-based reward multipliers
- Fully decentralized mining protocol
- Global seasonal contribution cycles

## 12. Conclusion

This document defines the governance rules for the Contribution-Mining System, ensuring fairness, accountability, anti-abuse protection, and sustainable economic rewards for the OGC NewFinity community.
