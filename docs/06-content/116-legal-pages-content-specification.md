# OGC NewFinity — Legal Pages Content Specification (v1.0)

## 1. Introduction

This document defines the structure, content requirements, formatting rules, and compliance standards for all legal pages within the OGC NewFinity platform.

These legal documents ensure:

- User protection
- Regulatory compliance
- Transparency of platform operations
- Clear rules for data handling
- Safe use of AI systems
- A trustworthy environment for all users

This specification applies to all legal pages published on the website or inside the platform.

## 2. Legal Documents Covered

### 2.1 Required Core Legal Pages

- Terms of Use
- Privacy Policy
- Cookie Policy
- AI Usage Disclosure
- Token Disclaimer
- Community Standards

### 2.2 Conditional Legal Pages (Future)

- Data Processing Addendum (DPA)
- Intellectual Property Policy
- Children's Privacy Notice
- Marketplace Terms (future)

## 3. General Formatting Rules

### 3.1 Structure

Each legal page must follow:

- H1 → Document Title
- H2 → Major Sections
- H3 → Subsections
- Numbered structure when appropriate

### 3.2 Writing Style

Legal content must be:

- Clear
- Formal
- Transparent
- Non-ambiguous
- Compliant with international guidelines

### 3.3 Tone Requirements

- Professional
- Neutral
- Avoid technical jargon when unnecessary
- Avoid marketing or opinion language

## 4. Terms of Use Specification

### 4.1 Required Sections

- Introduction
- Acceptance of Terms
- Changes to Terms
- Eligibility
- Account Responsibilities
- Prohibited Conduct
- Intellectual Property
- Content Ownership
- Challenge Participation Rules
- AI Interaction Guidelines
- Limitation of Liability
- Termination
- Governing Law
- Contact Information

### 4.2 Mandatory Disclosures

Must clearly explain:

- User responsibilities
- Content rights
- Allowed and prohibited behaviors
- Terminology definitions

## 5. Privacy Policy Specification

### 5.1 Required Sections

- Overview
- Data Collected
- How Data Is Used
- Legal Basis for Processing
- Data Sharing
- Cookies & Tracking
- Security of Data
- International Transfers
- User Rights (GDPR, etc.)
- Data Retention
- Children's Privacy
- Contact Information

### 5.2 Compliance Standards

Must follow:

- GDPR
- CCPA/CPRA
- ePrivacy
- Industry best practices

### 5.3 Data Rights

Include user rights to:

- Access
- Correct
- Delete
- Export
- Restrict
- Object

## 6. Cookie Policy Specification

### 6.1 Required Sections

- What Cookies Are
- Types of Cookies Used
- Why Cookies Are Used
- Managing Cookies
- Third-Party Cookies
- Consent Banner Guidelines

### 6.2 Consent Requirements

Must follow:

- Explicit opt-in for EU
- Revocable cookie preferences
- Audit logs (future)

## 7. AI Usage Disclosure Specification

### 7.1 Required Sections

- Purpose of AI Usage
- How AI Assists Users
- Limitations of AI
- Data Handling in AI Systems
- User Responsibilities
- Transparency Commitments

### 7.2 Requirements

Must clearly disclose:

- AI-generated outputs may be imperfect
- Human review recommended for critical decisions
- Any user inputs sent to AI systems

## 8. Token Disclaimer Specification

### 8.1 Required Sections

- Purpose of OGCFinity
- Utility Nature of Token
- No Investment Guarantee
- No Speculation Encouraged
- Market Volatility Warning
- Compliance with Local Laws
- Risks Associated with Digital Tokens

### 8.2 Legal Requirements

Must explicitly include:

- Non-security statement
- No financial advice
- Risk acknowledgment
- User responsibility

## 9. Community Standards Specification

### 9.1 Required Sections

- Code of Conduct
- Friendly & Inclusive Behavior
- Forbidden Activities
- Anti-Harassment Rules
- Youth Safety Requirements
- Reporting Process
- Enforcement Actions

### 9.2 Additional Rules

- No hate speech
- No explicit content
- No abuse of challenges
- No malicious activity
- No exploitation of minors

## 10. Localization Requirements

### 10.1 Mandatory Translations

All legal pages must be available in:

- English
- Arabic
- French
- Spanish

### 10.2 Translation Requirements

- Must maintain legal equivalence
- Verified by human translator
- RTL formatting for Arabic

### 10.3 Version Synchronization

All languages must use:

- Same version numbers
- Same change logs
- Same publication dates

## 11. Storage & Versioning

### 11.1 File Structure

```
/legal/
   terms-of-use.md
   privacy-policy.md
   cookie-policy.md
   ai-usage-disclosure.md
   token-disclaimer.md
   community-standards.md
```

### 11.2 Version Tracking

Each document must include:

- Version number
- Change history
- Last updated date
- Editor name

### 11.3 Archived Versions

Old versions stored in:

`/legal/archive/`
